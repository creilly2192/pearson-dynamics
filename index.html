Ocean Systems Engineer at ASV, LLC

David Pearson is a graduate of Florida Atlantic University and holds a Master's degree in Ocean Engineering and a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering. He currently works for ASV, LLC where he is constantly exposed to new challenges as they push the boundaries of the unmanned maritime industry. Working on a small, multidiscipline team on the cutting edge of innovation, David lends his education and experience to design, develop, implement, test, and operate unmanned marine systems for oil-gas and government projects offshore.

David's experience and knowledge in project engineering, along with his wealth of knowledge and expertise from the members of his team, provides him with a true holistic systems level perspective for solutions driven project development.
